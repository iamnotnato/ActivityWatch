NOTICE
======

This repository is a modified version (fork) of ActivityWatch
(https://github.com/ActivityWatch/activitywatch).

Modifications by: Alan Nato, 2026

Purpose of modifications: Adapt the application to accurately record the
total time spent on game development. Existing tools such as WakaTime only
capture a very small time range (editor-focused heartbeats with short idle
windows), which under-reports real development time spent across game
engines, art tools, browsers, and other applications.
